Mine Malkoc

Violin and Piano Instructor

She started her piano education at the age of 5 as a hobby and started her formal education when she got admitted to Hacettepe Ankara State Conservatory violin section at age 10. After graduating from Hacettepe University she continued her studies at Rotterdam Music and Dance Academy in Netherlands. After finishing her studies in Netherlands, she got accepted to Baskent University Academic Orchestra. While working in the orchestra she performed with other orchestras and chamber music groups along with solo concerts and lessons. In the meantime, she got her master’s degree from Baskent University Conservatory. After 15-years of orchestra and tutoring experience, she now lives in Boston.
